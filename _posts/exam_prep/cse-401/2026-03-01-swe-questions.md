# Software Engineering Previous Exam Questions

## Final Examination - 2021

1. 
	1. Define software engineering. Discuss the scope and necessity of software engineering. (5)
	2. Identify at least four basic characteristics that differentiate a simple program from a software product. (3)
	3. State five symptoms of the present software crisis. (4)
2. 
	1. What is software life cycle model? Explain the problems that might be faced by an organization if it does not follow any software life cycle model. (5)
	2. What are the phase entry and phase exit criteria of classical waterfall model. (3)
	3. Identify at least two activities carried out during each quadrant of the spiral model. (4)
3. 
	1. Why rapid development and rapid delivery is now the most critical software requirements? (3)
	2. List and describe the principles of agile methods. When you recommend agile methods for developing a software? (6)
	3. Define refactoring, collective ownership, and pair programming. (3)
4. 
	1. How do you define user and system requirements? Give example. (4)
	2. List and explain the metrics for specifying nonfunctional requirements. (4)
	3. What are the ways of writing a requirements specification? (4)
5. 
	1. During the requirements validation process what types of checks should be carried out on the requirements in the requirements document? (4)
	2. Draw and describe an abstract model of the 'traditional' testing process, as used in plan-driven development. (5)
	3. Define unit testing, component testing, and system testing. (3)
6. 
	1. What are the principal concerns of software quality management? (2)
	2. How do you define quality conflicts? (2)
	3. What are the benefits of involving users in release testing at an early stages of development? (3)
	4. Draw the block diagram of acceptance testing process. (5)
7. 
	1. What is legacy software? Why should we improve this type of software and how? (3)
	2. Write down the steps for level 1, level 2, and level 3 ER-diagram generation processes. (5)
	3. What are the difference between the analysis and data modeling techniques? (4)
8. 
	1. Do you think that motivating people is an important role of a project manager? Why? (3)
	2. Give some examples of common software project risks. (2)
	3. What are the techniques can be used to estimate software costs and efforts? (5)
	4. What do you understand by milestone and deliverable? (2)
